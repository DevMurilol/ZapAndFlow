## Project Metadata
PROJECT TYPE: High‑conversion landing page  
CLIENT: Zap&flow – Electrical & Air Conditioning  
GOAL: Generate a complete UI/UX layout + component structure + copywriting  
STYLE: Modern, premium, high‑trust, energy + airflow aesthetic

## 1. Brand Identity & Visual Direction

### Vision Statement
“To be the most trusted and preferred electrical and air conditioning service provider in our community — known for excellence, integrity, and making power and comfort simple for everyone.”

### Brand Philosophy
“We believe that reliable power and comfortable air are more than just services — they are the foundations of a happy home and a successful business. Every connection we make and every system we install is done with care, because we know how much it matters to you. We bring energy, speed and enthusiasm to every job, big or small. We aim to make everything run smoothly — from the air moving freely through your space, to the whole experience of working with us. We never cut corners, and everything we do meets the highest standards. We treat every home and workplace like our own, listening to what you need, and always being honest, fair and respectful. Keeping your power running strong and your air flowing right — today, and long into the future.”
